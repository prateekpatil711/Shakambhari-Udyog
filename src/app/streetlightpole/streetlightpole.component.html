Residential and commercial areas as well as parks, benefit from our Street Lighting Poles.

Decorative and traditional street lighting poles are designed and manufactured by our company for use in communities.

The backbone of street lighting, street light poles serve a variety of purposes ranging from providing appropriate illumination to beautifying metropolitan settings.

Shakambhari Udyog, as a street light pole manufacturer, provides a variety of poles with mounting heights upto 12 metres and various arm brackets.
