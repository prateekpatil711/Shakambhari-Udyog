Highmast Light Pole

A tall pole with lights attached to the top pointing towards the ground is known as high-mast lighting, and it is commonly but not always used to illuminate a highway or recreational area. It's employed where there's a need for a lot of illumination.

Shakambhari Udyog, as a Highmast light pole manufacturer, provides poles with mounting heights upto 12 metres. It also makes installation and maintenance of luminaires easier thanks to the geared mechanism that lower and raise the luminaires.Lighting load,tower weight, and varying wind speed are all taken into consideration while designing a high mast lighting tower.
